What is C3?

Chaotic 3 is the premier and oldest competitive collegiate hip-hop dance team based at the University of Southern California, with 20+ years of history. Originally founded as an artistic outlet for members of the Chinese American Student Association, Chaotic 3 has evolved into a well-known team that strives to foster the passion for dance from new and veteran dancers alike.
